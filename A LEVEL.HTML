What Is ‘A’ Level..?

  • An 'A' Level Computer Course is an advanced and second-level course in the DOEACC Scheme under Information Technology. It is equivalent to an Advanced Diploma in Computer Applications as recognized by the Government for job purposes. Additionally, the CompTIA A+ certification is another entry-level qualification in the IT industry. Cambridge International AS & A Level Computer Science is a course that encourages learners to think creatively and apply practical programming solutions.

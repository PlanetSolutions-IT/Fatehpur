What Is DCA..?

  • DCA or Diploma in Computer Applications, is a 6 MONTHS diploma course in the field of Computer Applications that involves the study of numerous computer applications such as MS Office, Internet Applications, Operating System, among other subjects.

    • OBJECTIVE OF THE DCA COURSE...

  • DCA will teach the technical skills needed to get a job in the IT industry. The course covers a wide range of topics, including programming, software development, and web design. It will also help develop problem-solving skills.

    • Duration:-

  • 6-12 MONTHS

    • Eligibility:

  • 10 /10+2 /ITI Certificate after 10/ 2nd year of a Government recognized polytechnic engineering diploma after 10.

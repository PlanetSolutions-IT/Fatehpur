What Is CCC+..?

  • CCC PLUS is a certificate level course that aims to equip the workforce entering into / working in government sector with the vital skill set necessary to cope up with the ever-changing scenario in IT sphere. CCC stands for Course on Computer Certificates, which is a program that teaches the fundamentals of computer and information technology to the common man. The CCC certificate is provided by the National Institute of Electronics & Information Technology (NIELIT) and aims to expand the digital footprint in the country.

    • Objective of the CCC+ COURSE...

  • After going through the course, an individual is expected to be equipped with not only the office automation skills but also with the understanding of the latest technologies and e-Gov applications.

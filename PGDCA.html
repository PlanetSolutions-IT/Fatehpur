What Is PGDCA..?

  • PGDCA is a 1-year postgraduate course that focuses on advanced theoretical and practical knowledge of Computer Science and computer applications in the field of Information Technologies. Students will learn about operating systems, Software engineering, database management systems, and web programming from the PGDCA syllabus.

    • Objective of the PGDCA...

  • You can learn a lot about computers which helps in making you a computer expert.
  • You get an extra degree, which can be useful for you in any job.
  • You can become a computer expert.
  • Completing the PGDCA at an accredited university allows direct entry into the second year of a Master of Science (IT) or Master of Computer Applications (MCA) course.

    • Duration:-

  • 12 MONTHS
